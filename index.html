üìñ How to Use

What is this?

This app helps Girl Scout troop leaders track cookie transfers. It creates digital receipts, logs transfers to your Google Sheet, and emails receipts automatically.

Transfer Types

Troop ‚Üí Girl: Distributing cookies to a girl for selling.

Girl ‚Üí Troop: Returning unsold cookies.

Troop ‚Üí Troop: Transferring between troops.

Girl ‚Üí Girl: One girl transfers to another.

Auto-Save Roster

New girls and troops are automatically saved to your roster when you submit a transfer. Next time, they'll appear in the dropdown!

Signature

The recipient types their name and checks the consent box to create a digital record of receipt.
